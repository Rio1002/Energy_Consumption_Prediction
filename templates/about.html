About Energy Insights

Energy Insights is a web application designed to predict total energy consumption using machine learning techniques. Our goal is to provide accurate and actionable insights to help users understand and manage their energy usage effectively.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations with the knowledge they need to make informed decisions about energy consumption. By leveraging the power of machine learning, we aim to promote energy efficiency and sustainability.

The Creator
